# Introduction to Planetary Science

Course materials for **WBAS002-05 — Introduction to Planetary Science**, a 5 ECTS mandatory course for 2nd-year BSc Astronomy students at the [Kapteyn Astronomical Institute](https://www.rug.nl/research/kapteyn/), University of Groningen.

**Lecturer:** Tim Lichtenberg

## Course materials

The lecture notes are available as a Jupyter Book at:

**https://formingworlds.github.io/IntroductionToPlanetaryScience/**

## Topics

The course covers 14 lectures spanning the full breadth of planetary science:

1. Introduction & History of Planetary Science
2. Planet Formation & Orbital Dynamics
3. Planetary Heat & Energy Transport
4. Chemical Differentiation & Magnetospheres
5. Atmospheres I — Composition, Structure, & Energy Balance
6. Atmospheres II — Clouds, Weather, & Climate
7. Planetary Surfaces
8. Planetary Interiors
9. Rocky Planets — Earth & Venus
10. Rocky Planets — Mercury & Mars
11. Gas & Ice Giants
12. Meteorites, Asteroids, & Comets
13. Exoplanets — Detection, Demographics, & Characterisation
14. Synthesis — Solar System in Context & Astrobiology

## Building locally

Requires Python 3.12+.

```bash
pip install .
make html       # Build HTML → book/_build/html/
make pdf        # Build PDF via pdfhtml
make pdflatex   # Build PDF via XeLaTeX
```
